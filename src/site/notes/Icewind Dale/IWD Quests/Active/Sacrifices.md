Each [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Ten Towns\|Ten Towns]] has a potentially different approach to making sacrifices to [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Auril\|Auril]] in an attempt to stop the [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Eternal Winter\|Eternal Winter]]

Has been held monthly for around 12 months by the start of [[Icewind Dale/IWD Sessions/Session 1\|Session 1]]

Tied to full moon?  [[Icewind Dale/Lore/Calendar and Moon\|Calendar and Moon]]

The larger towns sacrifice habitants by tying them up to posts naked in the cold.

There are lotteries run by [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Children of Auril\|Children of Auril]]

We discover that they can be bribes to change the outcome although a more hard core member is possibly killing those selected anyway [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Ice Knife Killer\|Ice Knife Killer]]
