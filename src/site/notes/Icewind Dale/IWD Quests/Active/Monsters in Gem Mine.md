[[Icewind Dale/IWD Sessions/Session 2\|Session 2]]
Speaker of [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]] [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Oarus Masthew\|Oarus Masthew]] persuades the group to help clear the monsters out of the gem mine which is the town's main income stream.

Offers reward of 50gp and a magic item as well as food/lodging for the night.

It's located only 30 minutes from town.

The group agrees and heads to the mine.

Outside is a sign Kobold's Only written in common, [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Rintario\|Rintario]] is suspicious that it is too well written for some kobolds.

The group head into the mine.

We find:
stone figurine with tourmaline eyes worth 10gp
miner's kit

There seems to be a creature under a walkway we are using but we don't investigate.

We turn back after encountering a bucket lift and try another way, session 2 ends with combat starting with two giant rats.

[[Icewind Dale/IWD Sessions/Session 3\|Session 3]] starts.

Combat beings with the rats.  We kill two, [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Pleic Holdir\|Pleic Holdir]] passes the last rat to the creature under the walkway which disappears.  It had been trying to destroy the walkway.
We descend the bucket lift.
We are negotiating with the Kobolds but Rintario casts a spell and the Kobolds attack.
When Trex (lead kobold) dies a ghost appears, we destroy the ghost by destroying the pouch that holds it to this world.
We adopt a kobold called [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Cucumber\|Cucumber]] who believes [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Caelum\|Caelum]] is linked to dragons.
We take a short rest while [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Phenrere\|Phenrere]] cooks up some fantastic rat.
[[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Cucumber\|Cucumber]] finds two tourmaline worth 10gp each.
We cross a rope bridge.
[[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Rintario\|Rintario]] finds a mind slayer skull with a gem inside [[Icewind Dale/IWD Items/Mind Flayer Gem\|Mind Flayer Gem]]
The party is split on either.
Grell surprises [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Freida\|Freida]] when she was left alone, it paralyses Freida.
[[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Pleic Holdir\|Pleic Holdir]] jumps the hole to save Freida avoiding certain death on a fail.
Session ends mid fight.
