[[Icewind Dale/IWD Sessions/Session 2\|Session 2]]
Speaker of [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]] [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Oarus Masthew\|Oarus Masthew]] persuades the group to help clear the monsters out of the gem mine which is the town's main income stream.

Offers reward of 50gp and a magic item as well as food/lodging for the night.

It's located only 30 minutes from town.

The group agrees and heads to the mine.

Outside is a sign Kobold's Only written in common, [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Rintario\|Rintario]] is suspicious that it is too well written for some kobolds.

The group head into the mine.

We find:
stone figurine with tourmaline eyes worth 10gp
miner's kit

There seems to be a creature under a walkway we are using but we don't investigate.

We turn back after encountering a bucket lift and try another way, session ends with combat starting with two giant rats.
