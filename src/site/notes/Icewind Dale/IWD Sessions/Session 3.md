Summary
The party continues to clear [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Monsters in Gem Mine\|Monsters in Gem Mine]], killing and eating rats, slaughtering kobolds and ghosts.  We adopt [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Cucumber\|Cucumber]] the remaining kobold.  [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Freida\|Freida]] is surprised by a Grell.  [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Pleic Holdir\|Pleic Holdir]] does a long jump over certain death adding to his legend.


Notes
The session begins in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Locations/Termalaine/The Gem Mine\|The Gem Mine]] close to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]] on the [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Monsters in Gem Mine\|Monsters in Gem Mine]] quest.

Some tough fights.
We slaughter the rats.
Try and negotiate with Kobolds but end up fighting them.
One is possessed by a ghost and we narrowly defeat it.
A kobold [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Cucumber\|Cucumber]] joins the group to serve in return for food believing [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Caelum\|Caelum]] has dragon powers.
After a short rest and some excellent rat cooked by [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Phenrere\|Phenrere]] we journey on.
The party starts to cross a bucket rope bridge.
[[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Rintario\|Rintario]] discovers a mind flayer skull with a gem inside.  [[Icewind Dale/IWD Items/Mind Flayer Gem\|Mind Flayer Gem]]
Freida is attacked when left alone by a Grell.
The party is slowed from helping by the bucket rope bridge.
[[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Pleic Holdir\|Pleic Holdir]] takes a huge risk leaping across the drop to the underdark to help his friend.  The legend grows.
