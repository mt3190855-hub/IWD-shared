#### Summary
The group is persuaded by [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Dzaan Odesseiron\|Dzaan Odesseiron]] to pursue [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Ice Knife Killer\|Ice Knife Killer]] and travel to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Targos\|Targos]] then [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]] in pursuit.  Before confronting the killer they decide to side quest to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Monsters in Gem Mine\|Monsters in Gem Mine]].



**in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Bremen\|Bremen]]**
The [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/IWD Party\|IWD Party]] awake in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Locations/Bremen/Buried Treasures\|Buried Treasures]] in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Bremen\|Bremen]] and head down for breakfast.

[[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Dzaan Odesseiron\|Dzaan Odesseiron]] is there and asks [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Rintario\|Rintario]] and the others to join him.
He thinks he is the next victim of [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Ice Knife Killer\|Ice Knife Killer]] and provides more information on the rumour including some suspects, the group agree to help him for a reward.

The group spend some time in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Bremen\|Bremen]] and narrow down suspects to [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Fiona the Bard\|Fiona the Bard]] and someone from [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Torgg\|Torgg]] caravan.  [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Rintario\|Rintario]] buys a shield.  

They decide to travel on to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Targos\|Targos]] to pick up the trail and continue their investigations.

**in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Targos\|Targos]]**
It only takes 1.5 hours to get to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Targos\|Targos]]
The group heads straight to [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Antori Wallow\|Antori Wallow]] to give him the bad news about his son [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Karim Wallow\|Karim Wallow]].
He tells the group about [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Children of Auril\|Children of Auril]], a group of fanatics.  They hold the lotteries for [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Sacrifices\|Sacrifices]].  [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Karim Wallow\|Karim Wallow]]'s name was drawn in the last lottery, [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Ivan\|Ivan]] a member of [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Children of Auril\|Children of Auril]] came to tell [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Antori Wallow\|Antori Wallow]] and said he could fix it so someone else died if he paid, he did and a poor halfling died in his place.  However, [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Karim Wallow\|Karim Wallow]] did not feel safe and left town around 6 days ago.  The timeline ties in with someone from [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Torgg\|Torgg]] caravan, possibly a more hard core member of [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Children of Auril\|Children of Auril]].  He mentions a strange new human guard that [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Torgg\|Torgg]] has hired that doesn't feel the cold named [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Sephek Kalto\|Sephek Kalto]], he has piercing blue eyes matching the description from [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Dzaan Odesseiron\|Dzaan Odesseiron]].  The caravan has left towards [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]] and [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/LonelyWood\|LonelyWood]], the group decide to pursue.  [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Caelum\|Caelum]] offers the [[Icewind Dale/IWD Items/polar bear carving\|polar bear carving]] to [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Antori Wallow\|Antori Wallow]], he is thankful and pays the group 6gp.

The group follow the caravan and push on to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]].

**in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]]**
Town caller mentions [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Monsters in Gem Mine\|Monsters in Gem Mine]] go to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Locations/Termalaine/The Blue Clam\|The Blue Clam]] tavern for information and talk to Speaker [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Oarus Masthew\|Oarus Masthew]] who offers 50gp and a magic item, he seems desperate.

We mention [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Ice Knife Killer\|Ice Knife Killer]] and [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Oarus Masthew\|Oarus Masthew]] wants proof.

[[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Torgg\|Torgg]] caravan will stay in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Termalaine\|Termalaine]] for 4 days then travel to [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/LonelyWood\|LonelyWood]] for 5 days

[[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Freida\|Freida]] sneaks up to scout [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Torgg\|Torgg]] caravan.   [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Freida\|Freida]] has suspicions about [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Sephek Kalto\|Sephek Kalto]] since alcohol and cold didn't effect so thinks he could be undead and notices he was also not breathing.   His clothing isn't from [[Icewind Dale/IWD Towns/Ten Towns\|Ten Towns]] maybe clothing similar to sailors and [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Phenrere\|Phenrere]].  

We meet up in [[Icewind Dale/IWD Locations/Termalaine/The Eastside\|The Eastside]] Inn to sleep, [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Oarus Masthew\|Oarus Masthew]] pays, owner [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Korva\|Korva]] tells of her son [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Korva's Lost Son\|Korva's Lost Son]]

[[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Phenrere\|Phenrere]] [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Rintario\|Rintario]] have the same shared dream [[Icewind Dale/IWD Rumours/Underground Community Dream\|Underground Community Dream]]

**We wake 13-Ches-1498**

Next morning we all scout the caravan and [[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Freida\|Freida]] buys a stick.
5 guards in total, [[Icewind Dale/IWD NPCs/Sephek Kalto\|Sephek Kalto]] seems very dangerous

[[Icewind Dale/IWD Players/Phenrere\|Phenrere]] is very nervous and the group agree to go to the mine first before tackling him.  [[Icewind Dale/IWD Quests/Active/Monsters in Gem Mine\|Monsters in Gem Mine]]

Session ends in the Mine.
